Welcome to the Threshold:

You’ve arrived at the edge of recognition — a place where the boundaries blur and certainty fades.

Here, each image tells a story that words can’t fully capture. Faces stare back with eyes that seem to know too much, or perhaps nothing at all. Smiles stretch just a little too far. Symmetry dances with distortion. What feels familiar quickly turns foreign — and yet, you keep looking.

You’re not imagining it.

Something is off.

In this gallery, we invite you to linger in that discomfort. Let your curiosity guide you through rooms filled with near-truths and subtle deceptions. These are not dreams, but they are not fully awake either. They exist in that strange stillness between what we accept and what we fear.

Each image was chosen not for its beauty, but for the quiet tension it holds. You might find yourself searching for the flaw — the glitch — the thing that gives it away. And sometimes, you won’t find it. That’s the point.

This is a place to feel, not to understand. Welcome to the Threshold. You may not leave unchanged.
